About Me

Hello! I am Katie - a content writer and digital strategist based in Edinburgh who has passion for helping create and develop brands both online and in the real world.

Experience

Content Creator & Researcher
CERT Scotland | November 2021 - Present
I work as part of the Outreach Team to reflect the team’s creative vision and ideas, including: designing posts and stories for Instagram, Twitter, Facebook and LinkedIn, creating content and resources (posters/stickers etc) for the Stealthing campaign, and creating banners/headers for our event pages on Facebook and Eventbrite. This is a voluntary role associated with the Buchanan Institute, which campaigns for contraceptive empowerment in Scotland. CERT aims to promote a prepared, informed and supported experience of contraception, working to produce research-based change and a platform to champion the voices of others. Research within the group centres around improving the experiences of current contraceptive users and supporting the expansion of contraceptive options for everyone.
